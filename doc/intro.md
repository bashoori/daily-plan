## About Me

I’m a Data Engineer with 5+ years of experience building data pipelines that hold up under real usage.

My work sits at the intersection of traditional ETL and modern cloud platforms. I focus on designing systems that are reliable, observable, and easy to extend. Most of my experience comes from healthcare, retail, and enterprise environments where data is messy, inconsistent, and business-critical.

I care about how data actually flows through a system, where it breaks, and how to make it trustworthy for downstream use.
